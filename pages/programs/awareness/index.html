Awareness Program

The time to take action against climate change is NOW. The Awareness program by Reesykle is designed to incentivize and encourage people from all over the world to begin taking the necessary steps to raise awareness about climate change in their community. Users who submit valid awareness videos will be rewarded Sykle Tokens.

Climate Change at a Glance

42 billion tons of CO2 is emitted into the atmosphere every year.
Global sea level rose about 8 inches
(20 centimeters) in the last century.
The acidity of surface ocean waters has increased by about 30% since the beginning of the industrial revolution.

How to Start

Raising awareness about climate change can be done in 2 simple steps.

1

Grab your phone/camera and record a video of yourself saying:
"My name is [your-name-goes-here] and I'm ready to fight climate change."

2

Post the video that you recorded on your social media account (Instagram, Twitter, TikTok) and use the #reesykle hashtag.

2

Fill out the form below with a link to your post and your BEP20 compatible wallet address. Sykle tokens will be deposited after your submission has been validated (which usually takes up to 24 hours). Please note that for the video to be considered valid, you must follow the script mentioned above and include the #reesykle hashtag in your post. If you have any questions, please, do not hesitate in reaching out to us.

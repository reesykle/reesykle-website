What is Reesykle?

Reesykle is an eco-decentralized ecosystem, but it's more than that. Reesykle is an experimental ideological movement whose sole purpose is to inspire people to come together to fight against the ONE common enemy that poses a threat to all of us irrespective of our race, political views, socio-economic status, and opinions. You know that common enemy very well. Every day, he threatens our food and water supply, endangers species, poses risks to our health and that of our families, displaces whole communities, and pushes them further down into poverty. Politicians have long politicized this common enemy, proving, yet again, to those who give a shit about the only place that we have to live that we MUST take matters into our own hands. Reesykle aims to be the TOOL that we as a community can use to fight back against the common enemy that threatens all of our lives.

Staking / Providing Liquidity

A traditional method of earning passive income in the crypto world is staking your coins. Once Reesykle releases "Bin", our proprietary decentralized exchange (DEX) , members will be able to stake their coins and provide liquidity and earn Sykles in the form of dividends.

Debit Card Transactions

The Reesykle Card will allow members (holders) to purchase goods and services from merchants. These sales generate a transaction fee that is traditionally set to 2.9% of the total sale amount + $0.30. The fee may not seem like a lot on a small scale, but on a bigger scale where millions of transactions are being processed, the revenue generated from these fees can reach millions. As per our decentralized nature, all of that revenue is money that goes back into the pockets of our members (holders) in the form of dividends. How much you earn is contingent on the amount of Sykle tokens that you own.

Reesykle Properties

One of the most ambitious milestones in Reesykle's journey to maturity is to design and build eco-sustainable properties and communities for rental purposes, thus creating the very first Decentralized Eco Real Estate Market (DERM). These properties will not be owned by a single entity, they will be owned by all members (holders) that make up the Reesykle ecosystem. The income generated from these properties will be distributed to members (holders) in the form of dividends on a daily, weekly, monthly and yearly basis. How much you earn in dividends is contingent on the amount of Sykle tokens that you own.

How to Buy Sykle

At the moment, Sykle tokens can only be purchased on PancakeSwap. In the near future, you will be able to buy Sykle tokens through Bin, Reesykle's decentralized exchange (DEX).

1
Create MetaMask Wallet

Create a MetaMask wallet on your Desktop, or iOS/Android mobile device. This will allow you to send or receive Sykle Tokens. Remember to set the network to the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) network. If you don't know how to set your MetaMask wallet to the Binance Smart Chain network, click here.

2
Send Smart Chain BNB to Your MetaMask Wallet

You can purchase Smart Chain BNB on the Trust Wallet using your debit/credit card. Make sure that you are selecting the Smart Chain Token on the Trust Wallet and not BNB. Once purchased, copy your MetaMask wallet address, paste it into Trust Wallet and send your Smart Chain BNBs.

3
Connect Your Wallet to PancakeSwap

This process is slightly different depending on the device that you are using. If you are on a desktop, simply head over to PancakeSwap and in the top right corner, click the button that says "Connect Wallet" and follow the instructions. If you are on a mobile device, head over to the MetaMask app, go to setting (top left corner) -> browser, then type in pancakeswap.finance in the address bar. Then tap "Connect Wallet" in the top right corner and follow the instructions.

4
Buy Sykle Tokens

Now that you have successfully created a wallet and connected it to PancakeSwap, it's time to buy Sykle Tokens! Navigate to Exchange on PancakeSwap, set your From currency to BNB and enter the amount of BNB that you want to swap for Sykle tokens. Then tap/click Select a Currency, tap/click Manage Tokens, tap/click the Tokens tab, and copy paste this address in the input field: 0x5c25b256dbe756538265e5fc05b99ed49a59c986. Once the token loads, tap/click import, follow the instructions on the screen. Don't forget to set your slippage tolerance to 17%. Once you are ready to buy, tap/click swap and you will officially be part of the Reesykle movement.

How Does Reesykle Help The Environment?

At its core, the Reesykle ecosystem is all about balance. Every feature that we as a community implement has to take an eco-sustainable first approach. We need to think about how we can leverage whatever feature we want to implement to counter climate change, promote eco-friendliness and/or anything related to reducing the disastrous footprints that we as humans have left behind. At the moment of this writing, we are working on implementing a feature that allows us to tax a fee on every transaction that is going to be contributed to the Reesykle Eco-Fund, which is going to be used to fund important environmental causes determined by what the community deems important. For example, planting trees, beach cleanups, awareness campaigns and much more.

What is Reesykle Properties?

Reesykle Properties (RP) is Reesykle's real-estate program that aims to purchase properties and remodel them to become more environmentally friend (think solar panels, etc.,) or build new environmentally friendly properties. These properties generate dividends for token holders every day, week or month (depending on the duration of the tenants lease/stay). All profits made from these properties (minus maintenance and administrative fees) are distributed to Sykle holders.

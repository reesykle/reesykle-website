Frequently Asked Questions

How Many $SYKLE Tokens Are in Circulation?

There is an initial supply of 2 quadrillion $SYCLE tokens.

How Does Reesykle Help The Environment?

There are many ways that Reesykle contributes to the enrichment of the environment. One of them is frequently donating to environmental organizations. We're also working on establishing partnerships with eco-friendly brands and other organizations to promote eco-friendliness beyond just planting trees. We want to encourage eco-living as a sustainable lifestyle by making the transition to living a sustainable lifestyle as simple as possible.

Does Reesykle Offer Rewards?

Yes. Reesykle's native token, $SYCLE, distributes 4% of every transaction to holders. The team is also working on Reesykle's next-gen passive income system to allow $SYCLE holders to stake their tokens in income-generating real-estate projects.

What is Reesykle Properties?

Reesykle purchases properties with funds staked by $SYCLE holders. These properties are remodeled into sustainable/eco-friendly properties and rented out to tenants for profit. Each day, week, or month, we distribute the profits generated by renting the properties to holders who staked their $SYCLE tokens.

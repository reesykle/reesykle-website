What is Reesykle?

Reesykle is an eco-decentralized eco-system that contributes to the restoration and preservation of nature with every transaction. The goal of the Reesykle eco-system is to correct humanity's past mistakes by implementing eco-sustainable solutions while also rewarding its holders.

Besides Reesykle's eco-sustainable nature, the Reesykle eco-system is also rewarding. It is made possible by the innovative approaches taken by the Reesykle community to create utility in industries that play important roles in our everyday lives, such as the Payments Industry, Real Estate Industry, and so on.

Community is the fuel that powers the Reesykle Eco-system. Holders of Reesykle's native currency (Sykle) have a say (via Reesykle's governance system) in important decisions that determine the future of Reesykle.

Every transaction within the Reesykle Ecosystem contributes to the Reesykle Fund, which is used to carry out eco-friendly tasks such as planting trees, beach cleanups, donating to organizations that are fighting climate change, or funding startups that are coming up with innovative solutions to counter climate change or establish and promote eco-sustainability.

The Reesykle Ecosystem is fully transparent, meaning that anyone can take a peek under the hood and see what's going on. Even our source code is public and open-sourced, which allows us to fully harness the power of the community.

Staking / Providing Liquidity

A traditional method of earning passive income in the crypto world is staking your coins. Once Reesykle releases "Bin", our proprietary decentralized exchange (DEX) , members will be able to stake their coins and provide liquidity and earn Sykles in the form of dividends.

Debit Card Transactions

The Reesykle Card will allow members (holders) to purchase goods and services from merchants. These sales generate a transaction fee that is traditionally set to 2.9% of the total sale amount + $0.30. The fee may not seem like a lot on a small scale, but on a bigger scale where millions of transactions are being processed, the revenue generated from these fees can reach millions. As per our decentralized nature, all of that revenue is money that goes back into the pockets of our members (holders) in the form of dividends. How much you earn is contingent on the amount of Sykle tokens that you own.

Reesykle Properties

One of the most ambitious milestones in Reesykle's journey to maturity is to design and build eco-sustainable properties and communities for rental purposes, thus creating the very first Decentralized Eco Real Estate Market (DERM). These properties will not be owned by a single entity, they will be owned by all members (holders) that make up the Reesykle Ecosystem. The income generated from these properties will be distributed to members (holders) in the form of dividends on a daily, weekly, monthly and yearly basis. How much you earn in dividends is contingent on the amount of Sykle tokens that you own.

Reesykle Ventures

Reesykle is all about disruption and innovation. If we want to correct past and current mistakes that have led us to the current climate and political situation that we find ourselves in, we are going to have to start thinking differently, we can't afford not to! The goal of the Reesykle Venture rewards program aims to achieve just that, think differently, disrupt, and innovate dinosaur industries that have been holding us back from leaping into the next cycle of humanity. Reesykle Ventures is a decentralized venture capital system that allows Reesykle to fund and support startups that are coming up with innovative solutions to fight climate change, promote eco-sustainability through their products and much more. As always, per our decentralized nature, whatever profit is generated from these ventures is distributed to every member (holder).

The seedling stage is all about decentralization and community. How to Buy Sykle

At the moment, Sykle tokens can only be purchased via UniSwap. In the near future, you will be able to buy Sykle tokens through Bin, Reesykle's decentralized exchange (DEX).

1
Create MetaMask Wallet

Create a MetaMask wallet on your Desktop, or iOS/Android mobile device. This will allow you to send or receive Sykle. Remember to set the network to Ethereum Mainnet

2
Send ETH to Your Wallet

You can buy Ethereum (ETH) directly on MetaMask or transfer it to your MetaMask Wallet from exchanges like Coinbase, Binance, etc.

3
Connect Your Wallet to UniSwap

Connect your wallet to UniSwap by clicking "Connect Wallet" in the top right corner of the UniSwap DEX.

4
Swap Your ETH for Sykle

Set ETH as the primary token to swap, then click on select a token. On the next screen, click manage token lists, then tokens, and enter Sykle's contract address. Once these steps are completed, you'll be able to buy as many Sykle tokens as your heart desires.

What Makes Reesykle Different?

Eco-Sustainable

A percentage of every transaction is distributed to the Reesykle Fund to carry out our Eco-Sustainable crusade of restoring ecosystems that have been damaged by human activities. For example, planting trees.

Rewarding

Reesykle is a decentralized organization, meaning that every holder benefits from all the ventures that Reesykle embarks on. For example, receiving instant dividends from debit card transactions, or a monthly cut from the rent paid by tenants residing in one of our future Reesykle properties.

Innovative

Reesykle is all about disrupting industries using innovative Eco-Sustainable approaches and generating passive incomes for our holders.

Staking / Providing Liquidity

A traditional method of earning passive income in the crypto world is staking your coins. Once Reesykle releases "Bin", our proprietary decentralized exchange (DEX) , members will be able to stake their coins and provide liquidity and earn Sykles in the form of dividends.

Debit Card Transactions

The Reesykle Card will allow members (holders) to purchase goods and services from merchants. These sales generate a transaction fee that is traditionally set to 2.9% of the total sale amount + $0.30. The fee may not seem like a lot on a small scale, but on a bigger scale where millions of transactions are being processed, the revenue generated from these fees can reach millions. As per our decentralized nature, all of that revenue is money that goes back into the pockets of our members (holders) in the form of dividends. How much you earn is contingent on the amount of Sykle tokens that you own.

Reesykle Properties

One of the most ambitious milestones in Reesykle's journey to maturity is to design and build eco-sustainable properties and communities for rental purposes, thus creating the very first Decentralized Eco Real Estate Market (DERM). These properties will not be owned by a single entity, they will be owned by all members (holders) that make up the Reesykle .rsk-animated-gradient-card. The income generated from these properties will be distributed to members (holders) in the form of dividends on a daily, weekly, monthly and yearly basis. How much you earn in dividends is contingent on the amount of Sykle tokens that you own.

Reesykle is all about disruption and innovation. To build trust within the community, our founding father decided to lock our liquidity tokens on Mudra for 10 years! You can check out the certificate here.

How Many Sykle Tokens Are in Circulation?

There was an initial supply of 1 Quadrillion SykleTokens in circulation. We locked 50% of the supply for liquidity purposes on PancakeSwap, distributed some tokens to the founding members and early investors, and burned the remaining supply.

How Does Reesykle Help The Environment?

At its core, the Reesykle .rsk-animated-gradient-card is all about balance. Every feature that we as a community implement has to take an eco-sustainable first approach. We need to think about how we can leverage whatever feature we want to implement to counter climate change, promote eco-friendliness and/or anything related to reducing the disastrous footprints that we as humans have left behind. At the moment of this writing, we are working on implementing a feature that allows us to tax a fee on every transaction that is going to be contributed to the Reesykle Eco-Fund, which is going to be used to fund important environmental causes determined by what the community deems important. For example, planting trees, beach cleanups, awareness campaigns and much more.

Does Reesykle Offer Rewards?

Not yet. It is in our roadmap to implement reward features in the near future to reward our loyal holders via innovative and lucrative approaches.

Where is Reesykle's White Paper?

We tried our best to include as much information on the website to serve as the white paper. However, if the community demands it, we will be more than happy to allocate the time and effort to draft up the White Paper.

What is Reesykle Properties?

Reesykle Properties (RP) is Reesykle's real-estate program that aims to purchase properties and remodel them to become more environmentally friend (think solar panels, etc.,) or build new environmentally friendly properties. These properties generate dividends for token holders every day, week or month (depending on the duration of the tenants lease/stay). All profits made from these properties (minus maintenance and administrative fees) are distributed to Sykle holders.
